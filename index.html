Vietnam

Vietnam is a country of nearly 100 million inhabitants located on the eastern coast of the Indochinese Peninsula. It is the 15th most populous country in the world and is consistently ranked as one of the fastest growing. In 2020 the Vietnam National Administration of Tourism(VNAT) announced their ten year development plan and vision for the country through 2030. While world travel is still limited due to covid-19, you can learn about and experience some Vietnamese culture through their official travel website. Learn More Here

Vietnam boasts some of the most unique and fresh cuisine in the world.

Vietnam's national dish is a clean, clear, flavorful, noodle soup called phở. While it is a must have for any traveller in Vietname, be sure to check out some other national favorites such as bánh mì, bún chả, and chè.

Destinations

Although there are an endless number of places to visit in Vietnam, there are some more popular destinations. These are the cities most often visited by tourists and the ones most likely to give you the best experienc if you are a first time traveller to Vietnam.

  1. Ha Noi
  2. Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon)
  3. Da Nang

Sites

When you visit Vietnam, it can be hard to narrow down what sites to visit. Because of it's long and storied history, Vietnam is home to plenty of cultural heritage sites and offers plenty of unique and engaging experiences. These are some of the most popular sites.

  • Mekong Delta
  • Reunification Palace
  • Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum
  • Hạ Long Bay
